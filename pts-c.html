Potter Sprinkler Supervisory Switch PTS-C

    Key Features

    • UL and cUL Listed, FM Approved, CSFM Listed, NYMEA Accepted
    • Dimensions: 7 L x 3.75W x 3 D (including bracket)

The Potter Electric Signal, LLC Model PTS-C Sprinkler Supervisory Switch is a versatile solution for monitoring sprinkler system control valves, as well as securing gates and other applications. Its design is particularly effective for non-rising stem valves and challenging conditions. Housed in a Nema 6P enclosure, this switch can be safely installed outdoors, even in flood-prone areas like pits and wells, thanks to its sealed reed switch operation that minimizes contact corrosion. With a simple valve wheel turn, the plug is disengaged from the receptacle, ensuring clear indication of valve operation.
