Eaton Sync Circuit Module DSM-12/24-R

    Key Features

    • Uniquely designed to accept an independent strobe and audible input from the FACP and convert to a single output that connects toWheelock compatible appliances.
    • 3 Aere per circuit current handling at 12 or 24V DC
    • Low operating current draw
    • Compatible with all standard fire alarm control panels
    • Meets the NFPA-72 requirement for Temporal Pattern when used with the Series AS/AH and/or Series Exceder

Enhance the synchronization and control of your fire alarm system with the Eaton Wheelock Series SM and DSM Sync Circuit Modules. Designed to work seamlessly with Series AS/AH, Series RSS, Series RSSP, and selected strobe applications, these modules offer independent operation of synchronized temporal pattern horn and strobe flash. With the ability to silence the horn while maintaining the strobe flash, these modules streamline your system setup using just a single pair of wires. Choose from the SM-12/24 for Class B NAC circuit control or the dual output DSM-12/24 for Class A or two Class B NAC circuit control. Elevate your fire safety system with Eaton's reliable and efficient sync circuit modules.
