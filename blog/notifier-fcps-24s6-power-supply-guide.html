Notifier FCPS-24S6: Specs, Wiring & Compatible Panels

Overview

The Notifier FCPS-24S6 is a versatile power supply designed to meet the demanding needs of modern fire alarm systems. As a secondary power source, it effectively boosts the capacity of fire alarm control panels (FACP) by providing additional power for Notification Appliance Circuits (NACs). This notifier power supply is UL 268 listed and FM approved, ensuring compliance with stringent safety standards. Its ability to support both Class A and Class B wiring configurations makes it adaptable to a wide range of system designs.

Specifications

The Notifier FCPS-24S6 boasts robust specifications that make it a reliable choice for enhancing the power distribution within fire safety systems. Below is a detailed specifications table:
Feature Specification
Input Voltage 120 VAC, 60 Hz, 3.0 A
Output Voltage 24 VDC @ 6 A
Output Circuits Four Class B or two Class A
Battery Charging Up to 7 Ah batteries
Supervision Loss of AC, Low Battery, Charger Failure
Circuit Types CLIP, IDNET, SLC

Wiring Configurations

The FCPS-24S6 can be configured for either Class A or Class B wiring, offering flexibility in installation. Class B configurations are simpler and economical, while Class A provides redundancy, ensuring signal integrity even when a single wire is compromised.
Pro Tip:

When configuring for Class A wiring, always ensure that the return path is correctly looped back to the panel to maintain circuit integrity.

For Class B setups, the FCPS-24S6 supports four NACs, whereas for Class A, it can handle two NACs. The choice between Class A and Class B depends on the building layout and specific safety requirements.

Compatible Panels

The Notifier FCPS-24S6 is compatible with a variety of Notifier fire alarm control panels, enhancing their operation by providing additional power support. It integrates seamlessly with Notifier’s range of FACPs, including but not limited to the Notifier NFS-320 and the Notifier NFS2-640. When considering the integration of the Notifier Fcps-24 NAC Power Supply MISSING PARTS, ensure that the FACP supports the necessary communication protocols such as CLIP or SLC for optimal performance.
Pro Tip:

Regularly test the compatibility and functionality of the FCPS-24S6 with the fire panel to ensure uninterrupted operation during emergencies.
