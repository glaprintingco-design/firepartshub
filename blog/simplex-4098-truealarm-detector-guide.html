Simplex 4098 Detector Series

The Simplex 4098 series includes a range of detectors, each designed for specific applications within a building's fire alarm system. The series employs both photoelectric and ionization sensing technologies to detect smoke and heat.
Pro Tip:

When integrating Simplex 4098 detectors into an existing system, ensure compatibility with the system's central panel and communication protocol, such as IDNET or SLC.

TrueAlarm Technology

TrueAlarm technology is a hallmark of Simplex detectors, providing accurate and reliable fire detection. This technology utilizes addressable communication, allowing each detector to report its status to the control panel individually, reducing false alarms and ensuring timely maintenance alerts.

Installation and Wiring

Installation of Simplex 4098 detectors is straightforward, yet it requires precision to ensure optimal performance. The detectors support Class A and Class B wiring configurations, providing flexibility in system design. When installing, ensure that the detectors are mounted at appropriate heights and locations to maximize detection efficiency.
