Telguard Fire Alarm Control/Communicator TG-7FS-V

    Key Features

    • Certified for use on the Verizon 5G LTE-M network for nationwide coverage in the US
    • Serves as either primary or backup alarm communicator (can monitor existing phone line)
    • Powers directly from the fire panel's 12/24V auxiliary power supply or via plug-in transformer (included)
    • Supports virtually all alarm formats using dial capture for universal panel compatibility; Contact ID, SIA, Radionics Modem formats, Pulse 4x2/3x1, DMP, Sonitrol, etc.
    • Interfaces with panel over a single DACT connection for universal compatibility (use TG-PEM accessory when panel has no DACT)
    • Compatible with the TG-PEM for DACT-less connectivity to the panel (Fire Alarm, Trouble, and Supervisory panel outputs)
    • Link Supervision available in 60 and 5min frequencies to meet any AHJ requirements for Sole Path connectivity
    • 2-Supervisory outputs and 1-Trip input available

Enhance the safety of your commercial space with the Telguard TG-7FS-V Fire Alarm Control/Communicator. This advanced 5G LTE-M device serves as a reliable primary or backup solution for commercial fire applications, meeting all UL Standards for Commercial Fire systems. Designed specifically for Verizon networks, the TG-7FS-V transmits comprehensive data from intrusion and fire systems to central stations nationwide. For seamless integration, consider pairing it with the TG-PEM accessory for a DACTless connection to any panel. Prioritize fire safety with Telguard's cutting-edge technology.
