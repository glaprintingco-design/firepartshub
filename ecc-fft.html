Fire-Lite Firefighter Telephone ECC-FFT

Part Number: ECC-FFT | UPC: 783008112080 | Brand: Fire-Lite

~$1782.20
Enhance firefighter communication with the Fire-Lite Alarms by Honeywell ECC-FFT Firefighter Telephone. This reliable standalone system offers secure communication for firefighters with annunciation and control features for both local and remote telephone handsets. The ECC-FFT includes a built-in display and keypad for easy phone activation and trouble condition indication, along with 24 remote telephone connections that can be monitored individually or as a group. With an auto-program feature that recognizes existing firefighter phone jacks and allows up to ten users to communicate simultaneously within a building, this system ensures efficient and effective communication during emergencies. Additionally, the ECC-FFT is housed in a lockable cabinet with multiple knockout locations for easy field wiring, making it a versatile and essential tool for fire safety.
