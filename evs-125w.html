Silent Knight Fire Amplifier EVS-125W

    Key Features

    • SBUS addressable, up to four EVS-125w per system
    • Each EVS-125W is supervised and has four onboard audio circuits expandable to eight with the EVS-CE4
    • EVS-125W uses an easy to install Wire harness to connect the ECS-CE4 audio circuit expander
    • For use with the 5820XLEVS or 6820EVS

Introducing the Silent Knight by Honeywell EVS-125W Fire Amplifier, a powerful 125W intelligent amplifier designed for seamless integration with the 5820XLEVS or 6820EVS systems. This self-contained distributed amplifier is strategically placed near the area of protection, reducing wiring complexities while efficiently amplifying audio messages throughout the facility. With its own power supply and battery backup, the EVS-125W ensures reliable operation and supervision by the main panel for trouble conditions. Expandable up to eight speaker circuits with the optional EVS-CE4, this amplifier is a versatile solution for enhancing voice evacuation systems.
