Fire-Lite Fire Amplifier ECC-50DA

Part Number: ECC-50DA | UPC: 783008111984 | Brand: Fire-Lite

~$579.94
Enhance your fire safety system with the Fire Amplifier ECC-50DA by Fire-Lite Alarms from Honeywell. This external amplifier offers an additional 50 or 125 watts of addressable audio power, which can be expanded up to 1100 watts when connected to the ECC-50/100. With four built-in speaker circuits, expandable to eight with the optional ECC-CE4 module, this amplifier ensures comprehensive audio coverage. The ECC-50DA features field-configurable speaker output voltage options of 25 volts or 70.7 volts, along with a built-in power supply and backup battery charger for reliable performance.
