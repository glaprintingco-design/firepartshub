Kidde Synchronization Module EG1M-RM

    Key Features

    • Strobes are designed to flash at the same rate (synchronized)When used with a compatible synchronization source such as the EG1M-RM and EG1M synchronization modules, E-FSC and E-FSA fire panels, and EBPS series booster supplies.
    • 3 A rating (EG1M-RM only)
    • Delivers independent horn control for horn-strobes over twowire Genesis circuits. Two methods of horn control available: traditional NAC signal silence or normally-closed contact.
    • Convenient snap-on or single-gang remote models
    • Only one module required per circuit
    • Provides UL 1971 compliant synchronization for all strobes on the same circuit
    • Compatible with Genesis and Enhanced Integrity products

Enhance the synchronization of your Genesis strobes and horn/strobes with the Kidde Commercial EG1M-RM Synchronization Module. This dual-purpose accessory ensures precise synchronization of strobe flashes within 10 milliseconds of each other, surpassing UL 1971 standards. Compatible with Genesis and 2400 Series products, the EG1M-RM offers independent control for connected Genesis horns over a single pair of wires, making it a convenient and essential addition to your fire safety system. Upgrade your system today for optimal performance and compliance.
