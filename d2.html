    Key Features

    • Superb false alarm immunity with the latest sensor technology

Introducing the System Sensor D2 Smoke Detector, a cutting-edge 2-wire photoelectric duct smoke detector designed for versatile applications. With its pivoting housing, this detector fits both square and rectangular footprints and can be easily mounted to round or rectangular ductwork. Boasting airflow speed detection from 100 to 4,000 feet per minute, this model ensures precise and reliable performance by System Sensor. Enhanced features such as improved false alarm immunity, expanded operating temperature (-4°F to 158°F), and simplified installation and maintenance make this smoke detector ideal for even the most challenging duct environments. Experience seamless wiring with additional conduit space and effortless sampling tube adjustment for seamless integration.
