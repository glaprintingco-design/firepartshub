Illuminate your surroundings with the Edwards Signaling 48XBRMA120A Security Strobe Light. This dual-mode LED visual indicator is UL and cUL listed for both indoor and outdoor use, making it a versatile safety solution. With a durable polycarbonate/ABS blend base and a shatter-resistant polycarbonate lens, this strobe light is designed to withstand tough conditions. Choose from a range of lens colors and mounting options to suit your specific needs, and rest assured knowing that this beacon is immune to shock and vibration. Ideal for areas with high noise levels, the 48XBRMA120A offers steady-on or flashing LED modes, providing flexibility in signaling.
